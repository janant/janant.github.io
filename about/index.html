ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Anant Jain, a full-stack mobile developer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I'm currently a freshman studying Computer Science at the University of Southern California in Los Angeles, CA.

When I'm not coding, I like to explore and capture the world from different perspectives. Photography has long served as a channel for my creativity.
