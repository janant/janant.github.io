ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Anant Jain, a full-stack developer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I'm currently a junior studying Computer Science and Business Administration at the University of Southern California in Los Angeles, CA.

My fascination with computers began at a young age, when I spent many hours tinkering with my favorite video games to find glitches and exploit them. Since then, my childhood curiosity has led me on an incredible path to the field of software development and data science.

When I'm not coding, I like to explore and capture the world from different perspectives. Photography has long served as a channel for my creativity.
