Community Balance Privacy Policy

Community Balance stores favorite episodes on-device. Favorites are only added manually by the user by pressing the star button, either in the episodes list or in an episode detail view.

Favorites can be viewed in the "Favorites" tab in the Community Balance app. Favorites are simply used as a list of the user's favorite episodes. Favorites can be deleted, either by swiping left on an entry in the Favorites list or by pressing the Edit button at the top of the list.
