Rock Paper Scissors Mobile Privacy Policy

Rock Paper Scissors Mobile only stores user preferences. These user preferences consist of sound preferences (weapon sounds and clicks) as well as game-winning thresholds for Player vs. Computer and Player vs. Player modes. Preferences are stored on-device and can be viewed and changed in the Options menu, accessible by pressing the Options button in the main menu of the app.
