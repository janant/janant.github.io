Ignus Privacy Policy

User-provided information:
In order to use Ignus, you must sign up for an Ignus account. The account creation process requires your first and last name, as well as an email address. You must also create a unique username and a password in order to log into Ignus. You can also provide a profile picture and a cover picture which will be displayed on your profile. Ignus will only use your email address in case the service needs to contact you. Ignus will never send you spam, offers, or news, and your email address is never publicly visible to other users in the app.

Automatically created information:
If you request a payment from a user, send a message to a user, or write a review of a user, the time that the request or message was created will be stored. This time will be displayed to the other user to inform them the time at which the request, message, or review was created. Ignus will not store any location data or device identifiers.

Third-party access to information:
Others who own Ignus accounts can search for your profile and view your first and last name, username, and profile picture and cover picture. Users can search by name or by username. Users can also see your full name and profile picture on any reviews that you write of other users in the application.

Security:
We are concerned about safeguarding your information. We provide electronic safeguards in order to secure your information. Ignus will never sell your information to anyone, including advertisers. Please be aware, however, that no security system can prevent all breaches of data.

Changes:
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time for any reason. Ignus will notify you within the app of any Privacy Policy changes. You must agree to the changes in order to continue using the service.

Contact us:
If you have any questions regarding privacy while using Ignus, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at ignusapp@gmail.com.
